Why is our congregation separate from all the other congregations in our area?

As a congregation we have tried to be primarily a people that want to follow our savior Jesus Christ in all things. We do not want to do things because they are “traditional” or because “this is how we have always done it.”

So are you part of a denomination?

We are not part of a denomination. We want to be free to live under the authority of the Bible, God’s personal revelation of Himself. So we place a high value on reading, studying, and being changed by the Bible.

How do we want to live?

We want to bear fruit of the Spirit as described in Galatians 5. We want to be people and a group of people who abide in Jesus and live lives marked by love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control.

What can you expect if you visit us this Sunday?

You can expect corporate prayer, corporate singing, preaching, and conversation. Beyond that we may not look like congregations you have been in before.
